    Important. This is a planning model, not financial, tax or investment advice. It uses 2025/26 UK allowances (personal allowance £12,570, basic-rate band to £50,270, ISA allowance £20,000, pension annual allowance £60,000) held flat, simplified UFPLS-style pension withdrawals (25% tax-free / 75% taxable), and assumes UK legal-tender gold and silver coins (Sovereigns, Britannias) remain CGT-exempt. Investment growth is assumed, not guaranteed; crypto is volatile and gains above the £3,000 CGT allowance are taxable. Pension rules, tax bands and the state pension will change. Before acting — especially on lump sums, pension contributions and drawdown — speak to a regulated independent financial adviser and check figures on gov.uk.
